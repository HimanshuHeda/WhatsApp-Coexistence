WhatsApp Coexistence Setup — Connect your existing number to Cloud API

⚠️ Before you start: Make sure the WhatsApp Business app on the founder's phone is updated to version 2.24.17 or higher. Have the phone ready — you may need to scan a QR code.

What will happen:

  1. Click the button below to start Facebook Login
  2. Log in with your Meta/Facebook account
  3. Select your business portfolio "Octodo"
  4. Select WhatsApp Business Account "Octodo"
  5. Choose "Use existing WhatsApp Business App number"
  6. Scan the QR code from the founder's WhatsApp Business app
  7. Done! Both app and Cloud API will work on the same number

This page uses Meta's official Embedded Signup SDK. No data is sent to any third party.
